Project

Automatic Solar Tracker

An automatic solar tracker is a device designed to orient solar panels towards the sun to maximize energy capture. It automatically adjusts the position of the panels throughout the day, following the sun's movement from east to west. The system typically uses sensors, motors, and a controller to detect the sun's position and adjust the panels' angles accordingly. By optimizing the angle of incidence, solar trackers can significantly increase the efficiency and energy output of solar power systems compared to fixed-position panels.
